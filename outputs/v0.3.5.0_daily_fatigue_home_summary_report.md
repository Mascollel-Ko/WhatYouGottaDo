## Daily Fatigue Calculation

Each confirmed record is resolved by canonical stableKey first. Name fallback remains restricted by the runtime catalog, and legacy Exercise fields are used only when no canonical row exists.

Workload follows `progressMetricType`: load/reps use volume load, estimated 1RM uses the top eligible set, reps/time and session duration use their available record values, and quality-based work uses confirmed completion with RPE. The baseline search order is same stableKey, strength progression group, redundancy group, movement family, then a conservative taxonomy fallback. Missing distance and formal quality metrics use documented duration or completion fallbacks with low confidence.

RPE factors are 1.00/1.08/1.16/1.25/1.35/1.50 for RPE 5 through 10. Canonical LOW/MODERATE/HIGH/VERY_HIGH axis levels map to 0.25/0.55/0.85/1.00. `stressMagnitudeHint` is not used as a primary axis and cannot erase high local or joint/tendon load.

Six independent values are retained:

1. neuromuscular fatigue
2. systemic muscular fatigue
3. local muscular fatigue
4. joint/tendon/impact fatigue
5. movement-focus fatigue
6. recovery pressure

Recovery pressure is `durationWeight * (0.50 * max(other axes) + 0.50 * mean(other axes))`. Joint/tendon load cannot decay faster than MEDIUM when canonical joint stress is HIGH/VERY_HIGH, and jump/landing/direction-change impact cannot decay faster than LONG.

## Baseline And Profile Seed

Observed baseline uses median daily axis load with recent days weighted more heavily:

`observedBaseline = 0.65 * recent14 + 0.35 * previous14`

The initial profile supplies a temporary 40, 50, 58, or 62 baseline based on weekly frequency, experience, and recent training break. Seed weight is `(28 - observedTrainingDays) / 28`; it prevents division by zero initially and reaches zero after 28 observed training days.

## OFI And Labels

`OFI = 0.45 * meanAxisScore + 0.35 * maxAxisScore + 0.20 * highAxisPenalty`

Axis scores are personal-relative and clamped to 0-100. Labels are LOW 0-39, NORMAL 40-64, ELEVATED 65-74, CAUTION 75-84, and HIGH_FATIGUE 85-100. OFI 60 remains NORMAL and does not trigger a rest recommendation.

Bottleneck reasons remain separate: `JOINT_TENDON_CAUTION`, `POWER_REACTION_CAUTION`, `RECOVERY_DEBT_HIGH`, `GLOBAL_HIGH_FATIGUE`, and `LOCAL_GROUP_REPEAT_CAUTION`.
