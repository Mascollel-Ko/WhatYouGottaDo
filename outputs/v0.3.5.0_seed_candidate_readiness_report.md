## Required Generator Gates

1. Validate 215 rows and unique stableKey.
2. Backfill 56 blank source status/confidence rows from approved prior decisions.
3. Normalize or reject every production-field `*_REVIEW`, `NEEDS_SUBTYPE_REVIEW`, planning conflict, unknown, and unmatched token.
4. Translate sidecar enums to current Exercise enums through one explicit mapping table.
5. Emit candidate CSV/JSON plus a field-level diff against the current seed.
6. Keep every candidate row non-mutating until a separate review approves the diff.
7. Do not copy audit rationale, review flags, or mutation-safety fields into runtime metadata.
