## Cause

ProgramBuilder include/exclude selection and generation still had three practical gaps:

- Program include/exclude pickers searched only a short name-based option list.
- Preferred exercises behaved like score hints instead of hard include requests.
- Slot and beam candidate windows were still narrow enough for viable candidates to be dropped before context-aware selection.
- The widened candidate path could still displace required squat, hinge, and upper-pull anchors unless selected-main reservation and repair kept those anchors protected.

## Changes

- Reused broader active exercise catalog fields for ProgramBuilder include/exclude picker search.
- Kept empty-query picker results compact while returning all typed-query matches.
- Added a preferred hard-include pass after optimization so active preferred exercises appear at least once.
- Added HARD validation for missing, inactive, and preferred/excluded conflict stable keys.
- Replaced generic preferred-drop warnings with exact `PROGRAM_PREFERRED_*` outcome codes.
- Relaxed early inventory filtering so equipment and program-selectable mismatches survive as soft scoring concerns.
- Kept explicit excluded keys and direct sport sessions as hard gates.
- Widened slot candidate and beam windows to retain 30 candidates when available.
- Added beam scoring for preferred, excluded, repeated stable key, loaded strength, and soft equipment mismatch context.
- Preserved hard exclusions for direct sport, non-training, fatigue-only, analysis-only, and hidden planning candidates.
- Added required-anchor beam preference for target-slot and foundation/main-worthy candidates.
- Protected weekly selected-main reservations for squat, hinge, and upper-pull anchors.
- Prevented fatigue repair from rewriting required template anchors into recovery slots.

## Behavior

- Active preferred exercises are included unless missing, inactive, or explicitly excluded.
- Missing, inactive, and preferred/excluded conflicts are reported with exact stable-key outcomes.
- ProgramBuilder picker typed search returns all matching active exercises across catalog fields.
- Equipment mismatch and non-program-selectable metadata no longer kill candidates at inventory collection.
- Direct sport sessions remain excluded from normal generation unless explicitly preferred.
- Slot/beam selection keeps a wide candidate pool instead of narrowing to a small early top-k list.
- Required squat, hinge, and upper-pull anchors are protected through selection and repair.
